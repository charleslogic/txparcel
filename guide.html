How the County Maps Work

Every county map works the same way — here's what to expect.

← Back to maps

Pick any county from the home screen and you'll land on an interactive appraisal map. Each one shares the same set of tools and controls described below. (Inside any map, the ? button opens this same guide.)

The control buttons

The buttons stack in the top-left corner of the map. You start with three, plus a help button:

Locate me — jumps the map to your GPS location.
Switch base map — cycles through aerial / street views.
Parcels — turns the parcel overlay on / off.
Help — opens the in-map version of this guide.

Turning on the parcel overlay

  1. Tap the Parcels button (the grid of squares). It highlights to show it's active.
  2. Zoom in until you're close enough — parcels only draw once you're zoomed in to street level. If you're too far out, the corner reads "Zoom in to see parcels."
  3. Parcel outlines appear. Tap any parcel to see its details (owner, value, size, etc.).
💡 The button briefly shows a spinning icon while parcels load.

The two icons that appear after activation

Once the overlay is on, two more buttons unhide below it. Turn the overlay off and they hide again.

Value Compare (scale)
Show Values (dollar)

Show Values

Tap it to stamp every visible parcel with its total value (and $/sqft where available). Tap again to clear the labels.

Value Compare — this one is a two-step tool

Value Compare colors nearby parcels by how their value compares to one parcel you pick. The second step is easy to miss:

  1. 1Tap the scale button to activate it. It highlights, and a small options panel appears at the top.
  2. 2Now tap the parcel you want to compare against. This is the step people forget — activating the tool does nothing on its own; you must then click a parcel to set it as the baseline.
  3. 3Surrounding parcels recolor by value difference vs. your pick, each labeled with the +/− amount. Your baseline parcel is highlighted.
Compare a different parcel? Tapping another parcel now just opens its detail popup and shows the price difference — it does not change the baseline. To pick a new baseline, toggle the scale button off and back on, then tap the new parcel.

Options panel (top of screen):

  • Entire street vs. All visible parcels — choose which parcels to compare.
  • Total value vs. $/sqft — choose the metric. Switching is instant; no re-clicking needed.

Tap the scale button again to turn Value Compare off.

In short

Parcels → zoom in → tap a parcel for info.
For Value Compare: activate first, then click your baseline parcel.
